Omelete

Recipe provided by Simply Recipies wrote by Sally Vargas, and found on https://www.simplyrecipes.com/

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs grated cheese
  • 3 cherry tomatos
  • 2 tbsd chopped basil or herb of your choice

Equipment

  • Small 8-inch nonstick skillet
  • Spatula
  • Fork
  • Bowl

Directions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them until they are well mixed.
  2. Heat the skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour the butter into the skillet and let it melt.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and let it cook until the eggs are set.
  5. Pour the cheese into the skillet and let it melt.
  6. Add the tomatoes and basil and stir until the tomatoes are well cooked.
  7. Serve the omelette on a plate and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Enjoy!
